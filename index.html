About Me

Final year of MEng Electrical and Mechanical Engineering student at The University of Edinburgh, graduating May 2024.

Currently, I'm developing a control system for parkour-like agile quadruped locomotion using reinforcement learning, supervised by Dr. Steve Tonneau.

Last summer, my internship at the Robotics R&D department of Tesla in Palo Alto involved designing control systems, firmware, and testing actuators.

Earlier this year I completed an 8-month placement under the supervision of Professor Sethu Vijayakumar at the Edinburgh Centre for Robotics with the Statistical Learning and Motor Control (SLMC) Group.

In my free time I'm leading EUFS, the UK's #1 autonomous racing team since 2018, designing autonomous vehicle hardware and software for the Formula Student UK competition.
